Melo in College

Melo played one season at Syracuse University, and averaged 22.2 points, and 10 rebounds

He helped guide his team to thier first NCAA tournement title in 2003, he led the team in scoring, rebounding, minutes played,field goals made, and free throws made and attempted

In the championship game, he had 20 points and 10 rebounds, and got most outstanding player award

He originally planned to stay for two to three seasons, but he already accopmlished everything he set out to do so he decided to join the draft, where he got drafted third overall by the nuggets
