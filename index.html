The Legacy

Carmelo Anthony

He was drafted with the third pick in the 2003 NBA draft by the Denver Nuggets

He won all six rookie of the month awards but failed to win rookie of the year

While in Denver he led them to the playoffs every year from 2004-2010 and became the third youngest player to get 2,000 points

He was later traded to the New York Knicks with Chaucy Billups in exchange for Wilson Chandler, Raymond Felton, Danillo Gallinari,Timofey Mozgov,a 2014 first round pick and the swap rights in 2016
