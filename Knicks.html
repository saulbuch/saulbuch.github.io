Melo on the NY Knicks

During Melos first two years, he started a new era in the knicks, melo lead them to the playoffs twice and made the all NBA third team in 2011, where they played the miami heat and boston celtics,who both were the eventual champions,

During his third year, He became the first player in NBA history to record 50+ points with none being in the paint,He also became only the third NBA player to score at least 40 points on at least 60% FG shooting in 3 consecutive games, joining Lebron and Michael Jordan. He also made all NBA second team, and became the NBA scoring champion

During his forth year, He bace the 50th player to get 19,000 career points, and the 5th youngest NBA player to do that, He also resigned for a 5 year deal this season.

During his fifth year, he became the 40th player to get 20,000 points, and 6th youngest to do so, he was also named an all star starter this season.

During his sixth and current year he became the third player to score 10,000 points for two teams, the Knicks and Nuggets, he also got 24,000 points this season
